Bacon Mac & Cheese!

Description

Creamy, sexy, empowering. This Bacon Mac will completely satisfy the whole party.

Ingredients

  • General Purpose Flour (1 tbs)
  • Butter (1 tbs)
  • Whole Milk (1 cup)
  • Cheese (2 cup)
  • Macaroni (1 box)
  • Bacon (5 strips)
  • Ritz Crackers (half a sleeve or to taste)

Steps

  1. Cook Pasta according to box until just under al dente
  2. Prepare roux: Thoroughly mix flour and butter over medium heat
  3. Make Beschamel by steadily stirring in milk until sauce no longer coats the pan
  4. Make Cheese Sauce by adding shredded cheese to the Beschamel until desired consistency is reached
  5. Mix drained pasta with the Cheese Sauce, reserving a portion of the sauce
  6. Create an even layer of pasta on the bottom of the baking dish using half of the pasta
  7. Cover with a layer of the reserved cheese sauce
  8. Cover with remaining pasta, followed by another layer of cheese sauce
  9. Crush Ritz crackers in a plastic bag and mix with bits of bacon
  10. Top the dish with the crust/bacon mix
  11. Broil dish until topping is the desired color and crispness
