Chicken Fingers!

Description

Chicken Finger recipe via Binging with Babish guaranteed to give you complete control over the new economy you instill upon your community college. Do not abuse your newfound power or you may find your monkey released, your hair gummed, and your posse de-posse-ified.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Oil (48 oz)
  • Eggs (3, beaten)
  • Kosher Salt (1.5 tsp)
  • All-purpose Flour (8 oz)
  • Black Pepper (1 tsp)
  • Package Chicken Tenders (1-1.5 lb)

Steps

  1. Start heating oil in large, high-sided skillet to 375F
  2. Combine eggs and 0.5 tsp salt in large dish
  3. In another dish, combine flour, remining salt, and pepper
  4. Dredge the chicken -- dunk in egg mixture, then in flour. Repeat for double-coat
  5. Fry the tenders for 5-8 minutes or until internal temp of 165F
  6. Transfer tenders onto rimmed baking sheet, lined with paper towels
  7. Let chicken cool for 5-10 minutes and serve
